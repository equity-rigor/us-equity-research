## §5 The load-bearing risks
1. **Premium-multiple de-rate on cyclical-peak earnings.** ~21x FY26E (a ~7-turn premium to VST) on earnings inflated by PJM capacity cleared **at the FERC cap** ($329–333/MW-day; floor $179.55) and high power prices. The cohort re-rated up together and de-rates together; the highest-multiple name has the most to lose. Double de-rate → $150–175. *Mitigant:* stock already −40%; 45U floor + IG cap a full collapse.
2. **FERC co-location / BTM restriction (EL25-49).** FERC restricted behind-the-meter large-load co-location (the Talen/Susquehanna BTM structure was rejected) and blessed only front-of-meter, forcing data centers to pay full network charges — compressing the premium PPA margin; conversions "paused" pending the June-2026 framework. *Mitigant:* CEG's large sites are grid-interconnected (FTM-survivable).
3. **Calpine integration / bought-at-peak.** ~$26.6B deal added ~26-GW gas at a cycle peak (vs NRG/VST gas at ~7–7.5x), dilutes the clean-nuclear narrative, forces a ~4.4-GW PJM divestiture (LS Power, ~$5B, pending), and a ~25M-share lockup unlocks 2026-06-30. *Mitigant:* IG affirmed; $3.4B deleveraging; retail hedge.
4. **Hyperscaler AI-capex cut (correlated tail).** One Microsoft/Meta/Amazon/Google guide-down would compress the multiple, stall conversions, and soften power curves at once. *Mitigant:* 20-yr signed PPAs are contracted; nuclear scarcity persists.
